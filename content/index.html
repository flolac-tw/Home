程式語言學的研究對象是程式，可以想成是「寫得很詳細、可以一步步照著做的規則／流程」；程式不一定非得和電腦扯上關係，像 SOP（標準作業流程）和飛行員必用的 checklists（檢查表）就是寫給人執行（照著做）的程式。程式語言學的最大特點是用符號作為主要工具，精準（無歧義）簡練（不冗長）地表示出程式和相關概念，以及研究它們的各種性質。除了精準簡練以外，我們喜歡的符號有以下這些特質

  • 厲害：用符號把關於程式的事情好好寫下來之後，我們就可以用數學方法確鑿地談這些事情，用數學證明確保厲害的好性質，例如「符合某些條件的程式絕對不會出錯」。
  • 易用：符號是我們解決程式相關問題的工具，在符號設計上我們會有所講究，為各種問題量身設計順手易用的專門符號。
  • 內涵：好的符號往往有多個面向層次的意義，幫助我們用不同角度思考問題；
我們特別喜歡向數學和邏輯學借用符號，和他們長久累積發展的深刻概念互通。

In recent years, Taiwanese scholars engaged in basic computational science research have established research teams and collaborated with each other. Students who are interested in basic computing science must learn basic knowledge through a series of courses; however, domestic scholars engaged in such research are scattered in various schools, and it is difficult to set up a whole set of courses by themselves. Therefore, we invite scholars with similar interests to start classes together to train the next generation of research talents.

"Logic, Language and Computation" summer training camp hopes to cultivate students' ability to carry out basic computational science research independently. Since 2008, this workshop has alternated between the two themes. Since 2012, this workshop has officially become a summer course at Taiwan University. It is open to university students, but postgraduate courses are also welcome. Taiwan University students can choose courses through the National Taiwan University Summer Course Network, and students from other schools with school membership can choose courses through inter-school electives.

The research object of programming language is programs, which can be thought of as "rules / processes that are written in detail and can be followed step by step"; programs do not necessarily have to be related to computers, like SOP (standard operating procedures) and pilot The necessary checklists (checklists) are programs written for people to execute (follow). The most important feature of programming linguistics is to use symbols as the main tool to accurately (no ambiguity) and concise (not verbose) expressions of programs and related concepts, and to study their various properties. In addition to being precise and concise, our favorite symbols have the following characteristics:

  • Powerful: After writing down the things about the program with symbols, we can talk about these things with mathematical methods, and use mathematical proofs to ensure powerful good properties, such as "programs that meet certain conditions can never go wrong."
  • Ease of use: Symbols are our tools for solving program-related problems. We will pay attention to the design of symbols and design special symbols that are easy to use for various problems.
  • Connotation: Good symbols often have multiple levels of meaning, helping us think about problems from different angles;
We especially like to borrow symbols from mathematics and logic to communicate with their profound concepts accumulated over time.
